Torus

Science around
the hexagon

The purpose

A Branding & Naming project based around teaching science around France with as little hardware as possible. This was made possible thanks to Rennes 1 University. A fresh but impacting campaign was required, ranging from posters to a website they wanted to appeal to a large audience.

The design

After some thought we wanted to give a professional look instead of a homemade one to convey the serious nature of the project. Moreover we did not want to go with the obvious green of science but with a passionate red. The website is a one-pager that gives every information needed to find the team and participate in an event.
