ERN édition de la rue nantaise

From individual
to professional

The purpose

A complete redesign of the visual identity of an independant publisher based in France. The purpose of this project was to renew the logo and identity, and furthermore give a collection effect to the book cover layout to better perceive this editor in librairies.

The design

After many experimentations and client feedback. We decided to go with a simple and streamlined design. The layout is minimalist yet impactful with the logo at the center of it thus creating a modular cover.
